Why I Choose IT and my Future in IT Industry

LOW COST OF EDUCATION

The low cost of learning is one of the main benefits of selecting information technology over other careers. It does not require a four-year degree, in comparison to other careers. Without a degree, you can begin working in this profession, and you can easily earn certifications in a number of IT specialties. This means that by skipping college, you can save a lot of money.

THE FASTEST CHANGING INDUSTRY

There is always a big need for highly skilled individuals because the information technology sector operates at a speed that is significantly faster than any other sector. An aspiring IT professional like me nowadays can embrace advancement in big data, cloud computing, and cyber security. The more education and experience the IT professional possesses the more employable he or she will be.

GREAT INCOME

Information Technology Professionals earn a decent salary compared to other professions, which is a well-known fact. IT professionals typically enjoy good job security as well as high wages and beneefits. Some IT professionals who worked in computer-related occupations earned a median salary of PHP4,886,171.

WHY I CHOOSE IT?

The IT industry provides different career options as well as professional pathways for every industry area. There aren't any complicated entrance criteria, and no college degree is necessary. It is one of the global industries that is expanding more quickly than any other and provides a variety of job opportunities. Additionally, there are high levels of advancement, rapid employment, and excellent earnings. Other than that, I choose IT as my career path not just because of the salary, but the genuine happiness when you create something that benefit others.

MY FUTURE IN THIS INDUSTRY

In the future, I want to become a Software Engineer. While i was growing up, my cousins and other relatives is also pursuing this kind of job. They always encourage me to enter science, technology, engineering and mathematics field, and I'm very grateful I had that encouragement.
